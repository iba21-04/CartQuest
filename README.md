# Cart Quest

> **Shop, play, and have fun—what will you eat today?**

Cart Quest is a gamified shopping experience where users don’t just pick food—they play a mini-game while exploring different cuisines. From floating pizza slices to glowing taco icons, every interaction is designed to make the experience feel alive and engaging!

---

## Features

* **Choose Your Cuisine:** Explore a variety of flavors including Indian, Italian, Chinese, and Mexican.
* **Dynamic UI:** Dishes appear dynamically based on your selection, keeping the interface clean and reactive.
* **Living Animations:** Floating food icons, glowing buttons, and animated backgrounds create an immersive environment.
* **Interactive Virtual Store:** Click and drag items to "pick up" groceries from the aisles and add them to your cart.

---

## How to Play

1.  **Select a Cuisine:** Use the dropdown menu to pick your preferred cuisine.
2.  **Pick Your Dish:** Choose from the list of options that appear dynamically.
3.  **Confirm Selection:** Click the **“Continue”** button to confirm your choice.
4.  **Explore the Store:** Navigate the virtual aisles. Interact with floating icons and drag items into your cart to complete your shopping quest.

---

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
* **Animations:** CSS Keyframes (used for floating shapes, glowing effects, and smooth hover transitions).

## Future Roadmap

We have big plans for the future of **Cart Quest**:
* **Score System:** Earn points for selecting specific healthy dishes or high-value combos.
* **In-Aisle Mini-Games:** Introduce unique challenges triggered by specific grocery aisles.
* **User Profiles:** Save your progress, food preferences, and custom unlocked animations.
* **Immersive Audio:** Add sound effects for a complete arcade-style shopping experience.
